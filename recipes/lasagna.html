Description of the lasagna

cheesy and saucey and pasta-y

Ingredients of the lasagna

  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Lasagna pasta sheets

Directions for the lasagna

  1. make the sauce
  2. cover the bottom of the baking dish with sauce
  3. layer the pasta sheets and cheese and sauce
  4. baked for an hour at 375
  5. enjoy!
