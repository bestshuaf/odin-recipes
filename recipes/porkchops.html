Description of the porkchop

Very porky, and fragrant. Served hot. (danger! Not Kosher nor Halal)

Ingredients of the porkchop

  • raw porkchop
  • salt and pepper to taste!
  • oil

Directions for the porkchop

  1. Salt and pepper the pork chop
  2. Heat pan and add oil to pan, stirring gently
  3. Add porkchop to pan and flip regularly until brown and cooked to your personal preferences.
  4. Enjoy!
